# Product

<!-- impeccable:product-schema 1 -->

## Platform

web

## Users

The primary audience is hiring managers and recruiters evaluating Max Paardekam's software development work. A secondary audience is potential Nuxio clients looking for evidence of craft, ownership, and technical range.

## Product Purpose

The portfolio gives visitors a fast, credible view of Max's role, selected work, technical stack, and direct contact routes. Success means a visitor can understand what Max builds, inspect real project evidence, and contact him without searching.

## Positioning

The work itself leads. The portfolio connects Max's current software development education with real ownership at Nuxio and presents source-backed projects instead of generic capability claims.

## Operating Context

Recruiters commonly scan the site quickly on desktop or mobile, then follow project links to GitHub. Potential clients may move directly from featured work to email or LinkedIn.

## Capabilities and Constraints

- Single-page English-language portfolio.
- Featured stack: React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Vite, SQLite, and Node.js.
- Featured work: MAKE-A-THON, Red Dead Redemption II site, Museum De Pixel website, and Simple Calculator.
- Project details and visuals must be grounded in the linked public repositories.
- Dark mode and reduced-motion support are required from the start.
- The mobile layout becomes a clear single column below 768px.
- Visible copy uses hyphens only, never em dashes or en dashes.

## Brand Commitments

- Public name: Max Paardekam.
- Role line: Owner @ Nuxio & Software Development student @ Grafisch Lyceum Rotterdam.
- Voice: direct, specific, self-assured, and free of startup filler.
- The site should feel personal and experimental, not like a SaaS landing page.

## Evidence on Hand

- Public GitHub repositories with source code, README files, and visual assets for all featured projects.
- Direct contact routes: max@paardekam.nl, GitHub, LinkedIn, Hashnode, and dev.to.
- No testimonials, client claims, employment statistics, or performance benchmarks were supplied and none may be fabricated.

## Product Principles

- Lead with shipped work and inspectable evidence.
- Make the first viewport legible within seconds.
- Keep contact routes direct and easy to scan.
- Let expressive design support project comprehension.
- Preserve accessibility in both color modes and motion preferences.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA contrast in light and dark modes, preserve keyboard focus visibility, use semantic structure, provide useful image alternatives, and remove movement-based effects when reduced motion is requested.
